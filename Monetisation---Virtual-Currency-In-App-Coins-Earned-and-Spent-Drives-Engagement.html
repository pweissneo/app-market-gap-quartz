The Currency Architecture

Three-Layer Currency System

Layer 1: Soft Currency (Earned Through Engagement)

  • Earned through normal app usage: daily logins, completing actions, streaks
  • Examples: coins, gold, “XP”
  • Psychological effect: “I earned this, I’m not spending ‘real money’”
  • Used for routine purchases within the app
  • Creates an engagement loop: earn → spend → return to earn more

Layer 2: Hard Currency (Purchased with Real Money)

  • Must spend actual dollars to acquire
  • Examples: gems, premium tokens, “diamonds”
  • Gateway to premium items and experiences
  • Often only sold in specific bundle sizes
  • Whale currency: top spenders purchase in bulk

Layer 3: Subscription + Currency Combo

  • Monthly “Gem Pass” or “Coin Bonus” in subscription
  • Subscribers receive free currency monthly
  • Converts subscription from recurring cost to stored value
  • Makes the subscription feel like “I’m getting paid to stay subscribed”

Anchor Pricing Mechanics

The bundle illusion:

Small:   100 gems  = $0.99  ($0.99/gem)
Medium:  500 gems  = $4.99  ($0.99/gem) ← most popular
Large:  2000 gems = $14.99 ($0.75/gem) ← "best value"
         ↑
    Anchor price makes medium seem reasonable

How it works:

  • The large bundle sets an anchor: “2000 gems = $14.99”
  • The medium bundle seems comparable to the anchor: “$4.99 for 500 gems”
  • The small bundle seems too small: “$0.99 for 100 gems”
  • Result: 80%+ of buyers choose the medium bundle

What Non-Gaming Apps Could Do

The meditation app model:

Earn: "Calm Coins"
  - 10 coins for completing a session
  - 50 coins for a 7-day streak
  - 100 coins for referring a friend
  
Spend: "Premium experiences"
  - 200 coins: access exclusive guided meditations
  - 500 coins: unlock sleep stories
  - 1000 coins: 1:1 coaching session (once per quarter)
  
Buy: "Premium coins"
  - $4.99 for 500 Calm Coins
  - "Best value" anchor: $19.99 for 2000 coins

The fitness app model:

Earn: "Strength Tokens"
  - 20 tokens for completed workout
  - 50 tokens for hitting weekly goal
  - 100 tokens for consistency (5 workouts in a week)
  
Spend: "Premium content"
  - 300 tokens: access trainer video library
  - 500 tokens: custom meal plan
  - 1000 tokens: premium app feature unlock
  
Corporate subsidy: "Employer bought tokens for wellness program"
  → Employee earns tokens, spends on wellness rewards

The B2B2C + Virtual Currency Combination

The model:

  1. Employer subsidizes a wellness app for employees
  2. Employer buys a pool of “health tokens” for the workforce
  3. Employees earn tokens for healthy behaviors (workouts, meditation, checkups)
  4. Employees spend tokens on premium app features, co-pays, or rewards
  5. Employer gets healthier workforce → lower healthcare costs
  6. App gets B2B revenue + engagement loop

Why this is powerful:

  • Employer pays once, gets engagement + ROI
  • Employee gets gamified wellness + rewards for healthy behaviors
  • App gets B2B revenue AND engagement mechanics that increase retention
